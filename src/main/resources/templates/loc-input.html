Steps For Inserting A New Gym:

1) Go to Google Maps.

2) Find the gym you want to add.

3) On the location detail on the left, drag your mouse pointer from the upper left corner of the gym's name to just below the phone number field.

4) Copy the selection and paste it into the text box below. You should see the gym's website & phone number (if those were available to copy).

5) Now we need the latitude & longitude. Right click on the point of the map pin and select "What's here?" from the menu options shown.

6) At the bottom of the screen, the coordinates are shown as a hyperlink. Copy these coordinates or click the link and get them from the left menu. Paste them into the text box below.

Steps For Inserting A New Crag:

2) Change "Search Everything" to "Areas."

3) Enter the location name into the search field and click the magnifying glass.

You will redirected to:
a) the Mountain Project page for that location - (if so, skip to step 5.)
b) a list of results.

5) On the location's main page, drag your mouse pointer from the upper left corner of the crag's name into the image box on the right.
